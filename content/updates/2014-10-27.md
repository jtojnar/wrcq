The IRF Advisor Jonathan Sutcliff visited the WRC 2015 race area in September and was happy with the preparation progress. The organizers has released <a href="http://wrc2015.rogaining.fi/uploads/2/4/0/0/24004753/messagefromcoursesetter.pdf">Message from Course Setter</a>. The document provides basic information on terrain, expected weather conditions, map key specialities etc. Very useful esp. for those who have never visited Lapland.<br>
				The race area has been covered by snow now, the WRC course will be finalized in spring.
